Getting Here

By MRT

Take the North South Line to Kranji MRT and walk towards the TurfClub entrance on the right side. Follow the signage towards Derby 1 & 2 Function room.

By Car

There are complementary parking on first request first serve basis. You will need to paste the sticker provided by TurfClub on your vehicle IU before entering the carpark. Please note that there will be a non-refundable intelligence tax if you fail to do so.
